The beautiful scenery:
The scenery of Siri Paye is very elegantly beautiful. The atmosphere is very 
pleasant with wildflower beds and pure nature. The lakes at Siri paye are worth 
seeing and perfect to capture.It is mostly clouded with different shades of dark. 
It is so much pleasure to play cricket on its slopes.

Siri paye is not so easy to reach due to its unpaved roads. It is a high mountainous valley 
with an elevation level of 3.058 meters 
above sea level. It lies in Kaghan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa near Shogran. Its angels are up to 15%.

Makra Peak on a height of 11,200 ft. enhances the beauty of Siri with lake and vivid blue sky.
